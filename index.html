Questions, answered

Before you begin

What photo works best?add

A clear, front-facing photo in good light works best. Avoid sunglasses, filters, or covered faces.

How are my child’s photos used?add

Your photos and story details are used only to create and review the ordered book.

How long does the book take?add

Digital PDFs arrive within 24–48h after payment. Hardcover delivery can take up to 15 business days in Europe.

What if the likeness does not look right?add

Every book is reviewed before delivery. If the result clearly misses the target, we correct it.
