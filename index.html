Frequently
asked questions

How long does the digital book take?

Your cover spread PDF and interior pages PDF are delivered within 24–48h after payment.

Can I print my own book?

Yes. The PDF file you receive with the Digital Only version is optimized specifically for printing in a square 22 x 22 cm format.

Where do you ship hardcover books?

We are focusing on Europe first for hardcover delivery. The shipping address is collected securely during checkout for print orders.

How long does the hardcover book take?

Your digital PDFs are delivered within 24–48h. The physical hardcover book ships separately and can take up to 15 business days to arrive in Europe.

How are my child’s photos used?

Your child’s photos and story details are used only to create and review the ordered book.

Will my child’s likeness look the same throughout the book?

We build from an anchor likeness and manually review the final book for consistency.

What photo works best?

A clear, front-facing photo in good light works best. Avoid sunglasses, filters, or covered faces.

Can I preview the book before it’s finalized?

The book is manually checked before delivery, and the final PDF is sent digitally for your review and use.
