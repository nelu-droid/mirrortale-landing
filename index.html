Frequently
asked questions

How long does it take to receive my book?

Your ready-to-print digital PDF is delivered within 24–48h after payment. The hardcover version arrives in 10-15 business days.

Will my child’s likeness look the same throughout the book?

We build from an anchor likeness and manually review the final book for consistency.

What photo works best?

A clear, front-facing photo in good light works best. Avoid sunglasses, filters, or covered faces.

Can I preview the book before it’s finalized?

The book is manually checked before delivery, and the final PDF is sent digitally for your review and use.
