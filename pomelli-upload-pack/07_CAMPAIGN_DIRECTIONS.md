# MirrorTale Campaign Directions

Campaign goal:
Generate on-brand marketing assets that sell MirrorTale as a premium personalized children's storybook and keepsake gift.

Best campaign themes:

1. Magical bedtime gift
Show the finished book as the object that transforms bedtime into a personal adventure.

2. A story made from their world
Emphasize that the book can include names, favorite details, milestones, and family meaning.

3. Keepsake upgrade
Show the hardcover as a premium gift, shelf object, and memory-box item.

4. Fast digital delivery
Highlight 24-48h digital delivery for last-minute meaningful gifts.

5. Grandparent/gift buyer angle
Position the book as thoughtful, specific, and more lasting than another toy.

6. Everyday confidence stories
Use gentle routine framing: school mornings, bedtime, brushing teeth, trying new foods, potty training, or learning to be brave.

Recommended ad formats:

- Instagram carousel
- Instagram story
- Facebook/Instagram feed image
- Pinterest pin
- Product hero image
- Product comparison: Digital Only vs Digital + Print
- Gift reminder creative

Creative prompts Pomelli can use:

- Premium square hardcover children's storybook on a cozy bedside table, warm lamp light, deep teal and gold palette, magical illustrated cover, gift-ready, elegant storybook brand
- Open personalized storybook with softly glowing pages, moonlit bedroom mood, premium paper texture, forest green and gold accents, no visible real child face
- Gift-wrapped square hardcover storybook with ribbon and small tag, warm holiday light, premium family gift, magical but refined
- Product photo of a custom children's book cover standing upright on a dark teal velvet surface, gold sparkles, tasteful premium feel

Landing page sections Pomelli can create:

- Hero: personalized storybook where the child becomes the hero
- How it works: share details, create story, manual check, receive digital files
- Examples: finished covers and sample pages
- Pricing: Digital Only and Digital + Print
- Trust: manual review, secure handling, premium quality, fast digital delivery
- FAQ: delivery, printing, hardcover, shipping, personalization
