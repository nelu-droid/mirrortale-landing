# MirrorTale Voice And Copy Guide

Voice:
Warm, magical, parent-aware, gentle, specific, premium, and reassuring.

Personality:
MirrorTale sounds like a thoughtful storybook maker who understands family life. The brand is imaginative but never chaotic, emotional but not syrupy, and premium without sounding cold.

Use words like:

- magical
- personalized
- storybook
- keepsake
- bedtime
- gift-ready
- premium
- custom-made
- child-led
- gentle
- hand-checked
- made to keep
- family details
- wonder
- favorite things
- one-of-a-kind

Avoid words and claims like:

- fix bad behavior
- cure
- guaranteed
- therapy
- diagnose
- treat
- stop tantrums forever
- discipline problem
- naughty child
- unruly child
- perfect parenting
- AI miracle
- instant transformation

Copy rules:

- Lead with the finished book and emotional gift value.
- Mention personalization as craft, not as surveillance.
- Keep claims specific and believable.
- Mention delivery windows plainly.
- Use "parents" and "families" respectfully.
- Use soft confidence, not urgency pressure.
- Avoid fear-based hooks.

Sample headlines:

- A magical storybook made just for them
- Turn their world into a bedtime story
- A personalized book they can read, gift, and keep
- Make bedtime feel like their own adventure
- The story where they are the hero
- A keepsake for the shelf, the nightstand, and the memory box

Sample short ad copy:

- Create a premium personalized storybook with the child's name, favorite details, and a magical illustrated world. Digital files arrive in 24-48h, with an optional hardcover keepsake.
- Give a story that feels made for one child, not every child. MirrorTale creates custom storybooks families can read at bedtime and keep for years.
- From favorite details to finished pages, MirrorTale turns a child's world into a premium storybook.

Sample CTA copy:

- Start your book
- Create a storybook
- Choose Digital
- Choose Hardcover Print
- Make their story
