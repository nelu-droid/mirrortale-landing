# MirrorTale Visual Identity

Overall visual direction:
Premium enchanted storybook. The world should feel warm, luminous, crafted, and gift-worthy. Think moonlit forests, glowing pages, soft gold light, illustrated covers, tactile hardcover books, bedtime warmth, and magical details.

Primary colors:

- Night: #07161d
- Deep teal: #102b2e
- Forest: #1f4b3b
- Teal: #1c6d70
- Gold: #d99435
- Soft gold: #f0c76b
- Pale gold: #ffe6a8
- Cream: #fff1d0

Accent colors:

- Rose: #c97965
- Coral: #e08a5c
- CTA warm amber: #ffbd7a
- CTA coral: #f26f4f
- CTA deep red: #9d332d

Typography:

- Display serif: Fraunces or Cormorant Garamond
- Body: Alegreya Sans
- Fallback feel: classic bookish serif headline paired with readable, warm sans or humanist body text

Image style:

- Premium illustrated children's book covers
- Luminous fantasy scenes with warm highlights
- Deep blue, teal, forest, and gold contrast
- Magical book glow
- Hardcover product mockups
- Bedtime and family-gift atmosphere
- Square book format when showing the product

Layout feel:

- Elegant, editorial, and storybook-like
- Rich backgrounds, warm highlights, and clear product focus
- Avoid generic ecommerce flatness
- Avoid plastic, noisy, neon, or childish clip-art aesthetics

Best product-photo directions:

- Hardcover book on a bedside table with warm lamp light
- Book cover leaning against wrapped gift paper
- Open book with glowing illustrated pages
- Parent hands holding the book, without making a real child's face the subject
- Stack of premium square books with ribbon, bookmark, or gift tag
