# MirrorTale Business DNA Brand Brief

Brand name: MirrorTale

One-line description:
MirrorTale creates premium personalized children's storybooks where a child becomes the hero of a magical, gift-ready book.

Core offer:
Custom digital and optional hardcover storybooks for families who want a meaningful keepsake, a magical bedtime read, or a personalized gift.

Brand promise:
MirrorTale turns a family's details into a polished storybook that feels personal, beautiful, emotionally specific, and worth keeping.

Primary audience:
Parents, grandparents, godparents, aunts, uncles, and gift buyers looking for a thoughtful storybook for children around preschool and early primary age.

Positioning:
MirrorTale sits between a premium children's book, a personalized keepsake, and a family gift. It should feel more crafted and emotionally rich than a generic custom-name book.

What makes it different:

- The story is personalized around the child and their world.
- The finished book uses a premium illustrated storybook look.
- The final output is manually checked before delivery.
- Families can choose a fast digital version or upgrade to a physical hardcover keepsake.
- The tone respects parents and children. It is warm, never shaming.

Emotional territory:

- Wonder
- Recognition
- Bedtime closeness
- Gentle confidence
- Family memory
- Gift-worthy delight

Brand values:

- Personal without feeling intrusive
- Magical but grounded
- Premium but approachable
- Parent-aware and calm
- Trustworthy with family information
- Crafted, reviewed, and human-checked

Short brand narrative:
Every child has small details that make them unmistakably themselves: favorite animals, places, routines, questions, hopes, and little everyday victories. MirrorTale turns those details into a magical storybook families can read, gift, save, and return to.
